# 📊 AI Impact on Jobs by 2030 | Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This Power BI Dashboard analyzes the **impact of Artificial Intelligence on future jobs by 2030**. The dashboard provides interactive visualizations to help understand how AI is expected to influence employment, salaries, job demand, replacement risk, education levels, industries, countries, and company sizes.

The primary objective of this project is to transform raw employment data into meaningful business insights that assist organizations, students, recruiters, and policymakers in understanding the future workforce.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze how AI will impact different industries.
* Identify jobs with the highest future demand.
* Measure AI replacement risk across industries.
* Compare future demand with AI replacement risk.
* Analyze salary trends among different job roles.
* Understand employee distribution by company size.
* Compare AI demand across multiple countries.
* Provide an interactive dashboard for decision-making.

# 📊 Dashboard Overview

## 1️⃣ KPI Cards

The dashboard begins with six Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) that provide an instant overview of the dataset.

### KPIs Included

| KPI                 | Description                                         |
| ------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Average Salary      | Displays the average salary of all employees.       |
| Future Demand       | Shows the average future demand percentage of jobs. |
| Employee Count      | Total number of employees available in the dataset. |
| Performance Score   | Average employee performance rating.                |
| Job Satisfaction    | Overall employee satisfaction score.                |
| AI Replacement Risk | Average probability of jobs being replaced by AI.   |

These KPIs help users quickly understand the overall workforce scenario.

---

## 2️⃣ Future Demand Score by Education Level

### Chart Used

Area Chart

### Purpose

This visualization compares future demand scores across different education levels.

### Insight

* Bachelor's degree holders have the highest future demand score.
* PhD holders follow closely.
* High School education has lower future demand.
* Master's degree holders show the lowest future demand score in this dataset.

This chart helps identify which educational qualifications are expected to remain more valuable in the AI era.
